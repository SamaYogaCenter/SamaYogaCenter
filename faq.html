frequently asked questions

General Questions

Do I need to be flexible to do yoga?

Absolutely not! Flexibility is something you develop through yoga practice—it's not a prerequisite. Our teachers provide modifications for every pose, making the practice accessible to all body types and flexibility levels. Many people start yoga specifically to become more flexible.

What should I wear to class?

Wear comfortable, stretchy clothing that allows you to move freely. Yoga is typically practiced barefoot, so you don't need special shoes. Many students wear leggings or yoga pants with a fitted top. Avoid loose clothing that might get in the way during poses.

Do I need to bring my own mat?

We have yoga mats available to rent for $2. However, if you plan to practice regularly, you may want to invest in your own mat. We also provide props (blocks, straps, bolsters) at no extra charge.

Classes & Schedule

Which class should I start with as a beginner?

We recommend Hatha or Restorative Yoga for complete beginners. These classes move at a slower pace and focus on foundational poses. Once you're comfortable, you can explore Vinyasa Flow or other styles. Feel free to ask our staff for personalized recommendations!

How early should I arrive for class?

Please arrive 10-15 minutes before class begins, especially for your first visit. This gives you time to check in, store your belongings, set up your mat, and settle in before class starts.

What if I can't keep up with the class?

That's perfectly okay! Yoga is a personal practice, not a performance. Take breaks in Child's Pose whenever you need to. Our teachers will offer modifications, and no one is judging you—everyone is focused on their own practice.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before class starts without penalty. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in losing that class credit for class pack holders.

Pricing & Memberships

What's the new student offer?

First-time students can try unlimited classes for 14 days for just $49. This is a great way to explore different styles, meet our teachers, and find your perfect practice before committing to a membership.

Can I share my class pack with friends or family?

Yes! Class packs (5-class and 10-class) are shareable. This makes them perfect for gifting or practicing together with loved ones.

Do you offer student, senior, or military discounts?

Yes! We offer 10% off all memberships and class packs for students (with valid ID), seniors (65+), and active military/veterans. Ask at the front desk for details.

Can I freeze my unlimited membership?

Yes, we offer membership freezes for extended travel, injury, or illness. There's a small administrative fee, but we want to make your membership work for you. Contact us to discuss your options.
