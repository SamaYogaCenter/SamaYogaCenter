welcome to sama yoga center

Sama is a Sanskrit word meaning same, equal, equanimity — a reminder that there is more that unites us than divides us. At Sama Yoga Center, this philosophy is at the heart of everything we do. Our studio is a warm, welcoming space for people of all ages, levels, and backgrounds to move, breathe, and grow together.

We believe that there is more that unites us than divides us—the heart of our practice at Sama Yoga Center. Stop by for yoga classes, private sessions, events, and yoga teacher training.

FAQs

Do I need to be flexible?

Not at all! Yoga helps you build flexibility over time. Come as you are.

What should I bring?

Bring yourself and wear comfortable clothing. We provide mats, blocks, bolsters, and blankets.

Which class is right for me?

Beginners are welcome in all of our classes. You may wish to begin with yin flow or open level. Our teachers offer modifications for all levels.

Do I need to sign up in advance?

Reserve your spot in advance to ensure a spot in class. Walk-ins are also welcome when space is available.

When should I arrive?

The studio opens 15 minutes early to allow you time to put your phone down, clear your mind, and relax before class begins.
