## 🎯 Common Questions

### Q: Do I need coding knowledge?
**A:** No! Just copy and paste. The guides walk you through everything.

### Q: Will this work on my Squarespace site?
**A:** Yes! This works on all Squarespace plans that support Code Blocks.

### Q: Can I customize the colors?
**A:** Yes! The README.md has instructions for changing colors, fonts, and more.

### Q: What if something breaks?
**A:** Check the troubleshooting section in INSTALLATION-STEPS.md or README.md.

### Q: Can I use individual sections?
**A:** Yes! Use the individual HTML files instead of `complete-homepage.html`.

### Q: Do I need to upload any images?
**A:** The hero section uses an Unsplash image. You can replace it with your own.
