yoga teacher training

Join us this Spring for an experience that goes far beyond learning to teach. Our 200-hour Yoga Alliance® registered training — recognized by the nationally respected yoga teachers' organization — prepares you to practice and guide Vinyasa, Open Level, Yin Flow, and Meditation with integrity and equity for all.

This training is for you. You don't need to intend to teach. Many of our graduates simply wanted to deepen their practice, understand their bodies, and find community. Think of it as a retreat you return to every weekend — immersive, personal, and transformative.

what we will cover

  • Āsana study & practice
  • Anatomy & physiology
  • Meditation
  • Prānāyāma
  • Philosophy & Sanskrit
  • Teaching methodology

what's included

  • In-person group training sessions
  • Unlimited membership from registration to graduation
  • Comprehensive learning manual
  • Recorded lecture access
  • One on one mentorship
  • Opportunity to register with Yoga Alliance® at graduation

schedule

weekends, 12:30–3:30pm

April 18–19 April 25–26 May 2–3 May 9–10 May 16–17 May 30–31 Graduation: June 6–7

graduation requirements

  • Attend training modules or watch recordings
  • Complete 50 yoga or meditation sessions (at least 30 with Sama)
  • Submit and teach a prepared sequence
  • Complete an open-book exam at your own pace

Become a 200-Hour Yoga Alliance® Certified Yoga Teacher and immerse in your own well-being.
