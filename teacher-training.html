yoga teacher training

Join us this Spring for an experience that goes far beyond learning to teach. Our 200-hour Yoga Alliance® registered training — recognized by the nationally respected yoga teachers' organization — prepares you to practice and guide Vinyasa, Open Level, Yin Flow, and Meditation with integrity and equity for all.

This training is for you. You don't need to intend to teach. Many of our graduates simply wanted to deepen their practice, understand their bodies, and find community. Think of it as a retreat you return to every weekend — immersive, personal, and transformative.

the sama method

The Sama Method is our integrative approach to teaching yoga—rooted in the wisdom of the eight limbs of yoga and informed by modern movement science. Rather than preparing you to teach a single style, you'll learn to confidently guide Vinyasa, Open Level, Yin Flow, Meditation, and Restorative Yoga, equipping you to support students of all abilities, bodies, and stages of life.

At its heart, the Sama Method is about learning to attune—to your students, to the energy of the room, and to your own authentic voice. More than memorizing poses or sequences, you'll gain a clear framework for teaching, communicate with confidence, and develop the discernment to meet each student with presence, skill, and compassion.

Our greatest measure of success is the experience of our graduates. Every Yoga Alliance® review of the Sama Method has earned 5 out of 5 stars, reflecting the quality of our education, the depth of our mentorship, and the confidence our graduates carry into their teaching and their lives.

Your tuition is intentionally all-inclusive. From the day you enroll until graduation, you'll enjoy unlimited yoga classes, a comprehensive training manual, recorded lectures for review or make-up sessions, and individualized mentorship throughout the program. There are no additional class packages to purchase, no required book fees, and no hidden costs—only thoughtful support designed to help you thrive.

questions you might have

How much experience do I need?

We recommend about one year of consistent yoga practice before beginning teacher training—but you don't need to be advanced or especially flexible. The Sama Method is designed for every body and every level. Students arrive with a wide range of backgrounds, and we meet you where you are, helping you modify, adapt, and build knowledge, confidence, and teaching skills one step at a time.

What if I want to do this for myself and I'm not sure I want to teach?

You're in wonderful company. Many students join to deepen their personal practice, cultivate a meditation practice, gain confidence, and connect with a like-minded community. Whether you teach or not, this training is designed to enrich your life.

Is this training accredited?

Yes. Sama Academy is a Yoga Alliance® Registered Yoga School (RYS 200). Upon successful completion of the program, you may register with Yoga Alliance as a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT 200).

Will I feel prepared to teach?

Yes. Throughout the program you'll receive progressive teaching opportunities, individualized feedback, and personal mentorship. You'll graduate with the confidence to teach safely, communicate clearly, and create meaningful experiences.

Can I teach at a hot yoga studio after graduation?

Absolutely. While our training takes place in a comfortable room with gently warmed floors, you'll learn principles that translate beautifully to both heated and non-heated environments. Your education prepares you to teach with confidence wherever your path leads.

Can I attend from home?

Yes. We offer a live online training option, allowing you to participate from home while remaining fully connected to the group. You'll interact with faculty and fellow trainees in real time, ask questions, receive personalized feedback, and enjoy the same curriculum and mentorship as our in-person students.

Can I watch the recordings instead of attending live?

The heart of the Sama Method is learning together. Whether you attend in person or online, participation happens live so you can ask questions, receive individualized guidance, and practice teach with confidence. Online students join via Zoom with cameras on to create an engaging, supportive learning environment. If life requires you to miss a session, let us know. Recordings are available for review and make-up purposes, and we'll help you stay on track.

Where do the 200 hours come from?

A 200-hour teacher training includes much more than classroom instruction. The Sama Method is thoughtfully designed for modern life, blending in-person learning with guided home study, personal practice, observation, reflection, reading, written assignments, practice teaching, unlimited yoga classes, and one-on-one mentorship.

You'll integrate your learning over time—to create layered understanding, strong teaching skills, and lasting confidence.

What happens if I miss a weekend?

Life happens. We record the portions of training that are appropriate for review, and we'll work with you to create a plan so you feel supported and confident before moving forward.

How many students are in each training?

We intentionally keep our trainings small to create a supportive learning environment where every student receives individualized feedback, mentorship, and meaningful opportunities to practice teach.

Do I need to purchase classes?

No. Unlimited yoga classes are included from the day you register through graduation. There are no required class packages or additional membership costs.

Do I need to purchase books or materials?

No. Your tuition includes a comprehensive training manual, recorded lectures, and all required learning materials. We provide a recommended reading list and have many of the books available to borrow if you'd like to explore further.
