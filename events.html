training: pregnancy in practice

with Rebekah Jacobs

Saturday, June 27th 12:30–3:30 PM

A Prenatal Yoga Training for Teachers & Expectant Students

This one-day immersive is designed to support you to meet the changing body with clarity, confidence, and care.

Whether you are a yoga teacher or moving through pregnancy yourself, this 3-hour experience offers a grounded, intelligent approach to supporting the prenatal body — to ease discomfort, strengthen the mind & body and prepare for birth.

You'll learn to:
– Teach and practice with safety, awareness, and responsiveness
– Adapt movement, flow and shapes skillfully for each trimester
– Use breathing techniques as a foundation for regulation
– Offer grounding tools that cultivate presence and ease
– Understand key anatomy and create truly supportive rest

If you are a Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT), this training is eligible for 5 Yoga Alliance Continuing Education hours.

A refined approach to supporting pregnancy with steadiness, skill, and presence.
